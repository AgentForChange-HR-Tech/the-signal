Editorial Philosophy
"The Signal HR does not publish every day. Neither does any intelligence source worth trusting."

What makes a source worth returning to is not how often it publishes — it is whether what it publishes is worth reading. The Signal HR is built on that principle.

Live RSS feeds surface what is happening in real time. Quarterly editorial reviews keep curated content accurate. Weekly newsletter synthesis distills what matters. Human-activated alerts flag what is urgent.

The goal is not to be everywhere. The goal is to be right — and to be there when it counts. That is the signal. Everything else is noise.
